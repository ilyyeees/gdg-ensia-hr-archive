## Purpose

This repository is a collection of the work I produced and assisted with during my time as **HR Manager** for **GDG ENSIA**. It serves as a record of the documents, processes, and communication standards I helped establish for the club.

<hr style="border: 1px solid #E8EAED;">

## Repository Contents

The files included here focus on the operational side of Human Resources, with a specific emphasis on professional communication.

### <span style="color: #4285F4;">1. Email Communication</span>
This is the primary focus of the archive. It includes the various templates and scripts I wrote for external and internal correspondence:
*   **Recruitment Emails:** Acceptance letters, rejection notices, and interview invitations.
*   **Internal Updates:** Announcements sent to the team regarding meetings and events.
*   **Onboarding Messages:** Introductory emails sent to new members.

### <span style="color: #EA4335;">2. Recruitment Assets</span>
Documents created to support the hiring process:
*   Job descriptions for various club roles.
*   Interview questions and evaluation guidelines.

### <span style="color: #34A853;">3. General Documentation</span>
Other HR materials I worked on:
*   Onboarding guides.
*   Standard operating procedures (SOPs) for team management.

<hr style="border: 1px solid #E8EAED;">

## Note
These documents reflect my personal contributions to the club's HR department. All sensitive data has been removed, leaving only the templates and structural content.
